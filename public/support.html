How do I reset my password?

On the sign-in screen, tap Forgot password? and we'll email you a reset link.

How does trip location tracking work?

It's optional and off by default. Turn on “Auto-add places I visit” for a specific trip and Worldly adds the cities and countries you visit to your map. Only place names are saved — never your precise location — and it switches off automatically when the trip ends. You can turn it off anytime from the trip screen or your device settings.

How do I delete my account?

Open the Profile tab, scroll down and tap Delete account. This permanently removes your account and all of your data.

Where's my data stored?

When you're signed in, your travels sync privately to your account. See our Privacy Policy for the details.
