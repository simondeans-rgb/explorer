Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 June 2026

Worldly (“we”, “us”, “the app”) is a personal travel archive that helps you remember the places you’ve been and plan where you’re going. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. Questions? Email worldly@simondeans.com.

Information we collect

Account information

When you create an account we collect your email address, a display name you choose, and an optional profile photo. Authentication is handled by Google Firebase Authentication.

Travel content you create

The places, cities and countries you mark; “discoveries” (e.g. restaurants, sights) with your categories, verdicts and notes; trips and itineraries; journeys; and any photos you add. This is the content the app exists to store, and you control it.

Location information

Worldly uses location only when you ask it to:

  • Foreground check-ins: when you tap to check in, or open the app during a trip you’ve chosen to track, we read your current location once.
  • Background trip tracking (optional): if you turn on “Auto-add places I visit” for a specific trip, your device shares location in the background for that trip only so the app can add the cities and countries you pass through, even when the app is closed.

In both cases your location is immediately converted on your device into a country and city name plus the date. We do not store your precise GPS coordinates or a continuous location trail. Background tracking stops automatically at the end of the trip’s dates, and you can switch it off at any time in the trip screen or in your device settings.

Information stored on your device

Worldly keeps a local copy of your data (and, for background tracking, a short queue of derived city/country visits) on your device so the app works offline. If you’re not signed in, your data stays only on your device.

How we use your information

  • To provide the app: saving your travels, syncing them across your devices, and showing your maps, stats and stories.
  • To enable the features you choose, such as friend connections and trip collaboration.
  • To respond to you when you contact support.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use advertising or third-party tracking SDKs.

Sharing with other people

  • Friends: if you connect with a friend (by sharing your code and both accepting), they can see your places and discoveries. You choose who to connect with, and you can disconnect.
  • Trip collaborators: if you invite someone to a trip, they can see and edit that trip’s itinerary and the places added to it — including places added automatically if you’ve enabled tracking for that trip.
  • Your private items (journeys, photo captures, saved bookmarks, and trips you haven’t shared) are not visible to friends.

Service providers & third parties

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Storage) hosts your account and synced data. Their handling is governed by Google’s privacy policy.
  • Wikipedia is queried by landmark name to fetch a public photo and one-sentence description. These requests do not include your identity.

Data retention & deletion

We keep your data until you delete it. You can delete individual items at any time, or delete your entire account from within the app (Profile → Delete account), which removes your synced data — including your places, discoveries, trips, journeys, photos and profile — from our systems. You may also email worldly@simondeans.com to request deletion or a copy of your data.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. the EEA/UK under GDPR, or California under CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, export or delete your personal information, and to withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, use the in-app controls or contact us.

Children

Worldly is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Security

Data in transit is encrypted, and access to your synced data is restricted to your account and the people you explicitly connect or collaborate with. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We’ll revise the “last updated” date above and, for material changes, notify you in the app.
