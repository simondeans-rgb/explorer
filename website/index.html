The Atlas

Your world map, painted with everywhere you've been. Countries, cities and continents — with a Discovery Score that rewards going deeper, not just further.

Flight statistics

Import your flying history and see it come alive: flights per year, distance flown in London→New York multiples, airlines, airports and long-haul counts.

Discoveries & your Circle

Leave a verdict on a place in seconds. Your best finds flow to the friends you choose — and theirs surface right inside your trip plans.

Trip planning

Day-by-day itineraries with your trip crew, ideas from your Circle, and automatic travel logging while you're away — even with the app closed.

A beautifully typeset book of your travels — your photos, your routes, your story — exportable as a keepsake PDF that reads like it was written for you. Because it was.

Your own Wrapped: an animated, shareable recap of the year's journeys — the poster, the card, the video.
